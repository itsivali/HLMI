About Us

Harvest Land Ministries International (HLMI) is a non-profit, non-denominational, non-political, faith based organization founded by Dr William O.Ouma in 1989 with the aim of Reconciling and Transforming communities with the gospel of Jesus Christ. Also, to raise, develop, train and equip Christian and community leaders for effective transformational leadership, and to fight against discrimination and help provide support and care for the marginalized, less fortunate, and the vulnerable such as street children, orphans, widows, Persons Living With Disability, women, youth, and the Elderly. HLMIâ€™s aim also, is to address the issues of peace, inadequate human care services, poverty, and underdevelopment in such areas as socio-economic/micro-financing, livelihood, agriculture and food security, Humanitarian and Disaster management, medical, health, nutritional and psychosocial services, water and sanitation, Education, and Justice and advocacy especially in the area of violence against women and girls. HLMI works in collaboration with international Agencies, Developmental partners, Government sectors/ministries to ensure that better service delivery, and in a professional manner, is given to the communities we serve.

Dr William Ouma

Dr William D.O.Ouma was born and raised in Busia, western Kenya in Africa. He was born into a family of a businessman. His parents (Samuel & Getrude) lost their businesses through burglary and theft which opened them up to life of poverty. Despite the challenges of poverty, his parents were able to put him through school from primary to high school. Friends thereafter helped to put him through college up to PhD level. He received Jesus as his savior in 1978. Before his salvation, he suffered from multiple illnesses and was almost completely bedridden for approximately seven years. His parents tried everything to help him, from the best medical doctors to witch doctors, but he continued to get worse. When the doctors had given up and left him, totally bedridden, to die, he gave his life to Jesus. At that time Jesus miraculously healed him and placed in his heart a desire to serve the Lord. In 1983 he experienced a filling of the Holy Ghost which brought a big change in his life and ministry.
